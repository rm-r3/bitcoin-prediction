About This Project

How it works:

This tool uses a neural network trained on the historical Fear & Greed Index to predict Bitcoin market sentiment. The model learns patterns from thousands of days of data and classifies market conditions into five categories: Extreme Fear, Fear, Neutral, Greed, and Extreme Greed.

Training Data: Historical Fear & Greed Index data (2018-present) - Over 2,000 data points fetched daily from the official API

Live Data: Optional real-time Bitcoin price fetching from cryptocurrency APIs for making predictions on current market conditions

Model: ML5.js Feedforward Neural Network with classification

Data Source: The Fear and Greed Index measures market sentiment on a scale from 0 (Extreme Fear) to 100 (Extreme Greed). It's calculated from multiple factors including volatility, market momentum, social media sentiment, surveys, Bitcoin dominance, and Google Trends.

Disclaimer: This is a learning project. Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile and unpredictable. Never use this for actual investment decisions. Always DYOR (Do Your Own Research)!
